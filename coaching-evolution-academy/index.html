WORLD-CLASS TRAINING

Coaching is not for the faint-hearted. And coaches need to rise above circumstances to be able to coach others. We are proud to have some brilliant minds and true coaching talent attending our courses. Are you ready to be one of them?

Our Academy has already set the foundations for great academic reputation. The intellectual demands and high standards of the courses can make the whole experience rather intense. To ensure we maintain these high standards and at the same time facilitate a successful educational outcome, we guide our learners to increase their insight and self-reflection levels through rigorous training. A metaphor of Spartan education for physical strength and agility is what we do for the mind and coaching skills. The outcome is a “samurai coach” with the values and principles of an ethical professional

INTERNATIONAL COACHING PROGRAMMES

Coaching education has become evidence-based and is supported by research carried out by professional bodies, universities and foundations.

For our Academy, coaching is the evolutionary mix of neuroscience, positive psychology, sociology and applied philosophy. The art is the Socratic method of questioning. And this takes time to master.

The CE Int’l Academy is at the forefront of coaching education guiding you on your path to evolution.

EXPERIENCE.

We have designed coaching courses to facilitate the needs of every learner who wishes to become a professional coach and be accredited by a credible coaching body or any individual who wishes to develop themselves by learning a number of self-coaching tools.

Our courses are divided into Executive and Professional Education and Continuing Education.

Executive and Professional Education

  • Foundation in General Coaching Skills

  • Advanced Diploma in General & Specific Coaching Skills

Continuing Education

  • Microexpressions in Coaching

  • PsyCap: an Executive Coaching Tool

BENEFITS

The combination of face-to-face delivery and online material provide the utmost flexibility and quality while studying coaching models, tools and techniques.

  • Studying at our Academy gives you the opportunity to apply for an accredited course (our Advanced Diploma is conferred the EQA by the European Mentoring & Coaching Council).

  • You can study at your own pace as you still follow a structure that ensures you graduate on time.

  • You become acquainted to our own CE Int’l Framework for Results© and a number of unique CE Int’l coaching models.

  • The “buddy” coaching scheme will also help you enhance and develop your skills and achieve your full potential, both on a personal and professional level.

  • Studying online has never been easier with our state-of-the-art learning platform by LearnWorlds. It is a user friendly platform providing interactive face-to-face delivery with learners from all over the world. All you need is internet connection.

  • As a learner, you benefit from access to our extensive resources and online library, as well as the connection and bonding that is developed among all our learners from all over the world.

  • At the same time, the Academy provides strong support for your personal, professional and career development. With this in mind, it offers a well-being scheme, opportunities for social interaction, and a start-up guide (business plan, marketing plan, competitive advantage, market research) to help you set up your own business.

APPLYING

Our section on how to apply gives you all the information you need to make informed choices as regards the level and type of studying, entrance requirements, and fees. You will find a list of courses, how to combine them and the time frames you need to observe if you want to become a professional coach.

You will also find information on our Residential Coaching Charm which is a unique opportunity to practise your coaching skills while meeting and connecting with learners from every corner of the world. This international gathering can be funded through your fees and it takes place on a most beautiful island in Greece.

STUDYING WITH THE ACADEMY

Our Academy has already set the foundations for great academic reputation. The intellectual demands and the high standards of the courses can make the whole experience rather intense. Our teaching method is learner-centred and encourages self-directed learning. We also offer support in helping you to understand your own responsibility in your academic performance.

Our Programme Description (handbook) provides all the information required to familiarise you with our teaching and learning methods, assessment issues, tutor & course evaluation and what the Teaching & Learning Enhancement Plan covers.

The handbook also provides you with information on what to expect from the Academy, pre-course requirements, post course obligations, the content and structure of each programme, our complaints management processes and what is expected from you.

Our unique feature is the CE Int’l Advantage: it provides an outline of how the Academy delivers support and positive learning outcomes. It includes the Learning Plan, the Discovery Plan and the Engagement Plan.

MISSION STATEMENT

The CE Int’l Academy aspires to create a high achieving environment for all our staff and learners, that supports learning, critical judgement, openness, academic integrity and ethical standards. It is committed to the pursuit of excellence, the involvement of all staff and students in the educational experience and its improvement where necessary. It is also committed to the EMCC (European Mentoring & Coaching Council) and ICF (International Coach Federation) code of Ethics and the maintenance of the high professional standards of coaching internationally.

The Academy aspires to instil the coaching values to all its graduates and ensure that all coaching competencies as defined by the ICF and the EMCC have been mastered in relevance to the course of study chosen.

LEARNER SUPPORT FRAMEWORK

Our aim at the CE Int’l Academy is to assist you in developing critical knowledge and disciplinary competence relevant to local and global coaching contexts and on issues of professional, environmental and social significance. You will be responsible, global citizens fully capable and willing to engage with issues of equity, confidentiality, social justice and ethos.

The Learner Support Framework is provided by your Academy and the staff to assist you in this direction.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

The overall coaching course quality assurance processes are there to safeguard your investment in high quality course content, a transformative delivery and professional outcomes. The Programme Manual includes information on the CE Int’l Quality Assurance Policy which enacts upon:

  • CE Strategic Plan

  • Academic Board (Responsibilities & Duties)

  • Teaching and Learning Enhancement Plan

  • Teaching and Learning Roles and Responsibilities

  • SEC&T (Student Evaluation of Course and Trainer)

  • Curriculum and Teaching Quality Evaluation (CTQE)

  • Analytic Scale for Rating Coaching Performance on the Programme

  • Teaching and Learning Award

  • Learner Support Framework

  • International Agreements

  • Complaints Management

 Villa Kastraki

Villa Kastraki is a family run business in the Kastraki area on Naxos island. Located in the southwest of Naxos. Kastraki beach is 3 km of coastline with fine white sand.  It is an ideal location for those seeking a peaceful, relaxing vacation, enjoying the sun and nature, in a quiet place away from the noisy summer resorts. In the region you can visit the  ruins of the Mycenaean acropolis and the tower of Oskelou.

We - the Papaioannou family- have the philosophy of work-life-balance. We welcome you with our traditional Naxian hospitality. All the rooms are exquisitely decorated  and very clean. Villa Kastraki offers 7 rooms with balcony, most of them with a stunning ( or breathtaking) sea & land view. All the rooms are fully functional and equipped with a private bathroom, air-conditioning, satellite TV, music channels, refrigerator, safe, WiFi.
